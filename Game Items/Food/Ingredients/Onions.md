# Onions

**Onions** a common vegetable found on **Earth**.

# Obtaining

**Onions** is obtained from the [**Hydroponics Pod**]() located in the **Bio-Dome** sector of the base.
 
**Spawn Rate :** **1 per 10min**

# Usage

The **Onions** is used to cook food.
