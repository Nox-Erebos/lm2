# Strawberry

**Strawberry** a common fruit found on **Earth**.

# Obtaining

**Strawberry** is obtained from the [**Hydroponics Pod**]() located in the **Bio-Dome** sector of the base.
 
**Spawn Rate :** **1 per 10min**

# Usage

The **Strawberry** is used to cook food.
