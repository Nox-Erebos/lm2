**- 1/3** [**Artificial Flavoring Ingredients**](https://github.com/AlphaMC0/Lone-Martian/blob/main/Ingredients/Strawberry.md)

**Strawberry Spinach Salad** is used to instantly heal the 250hp and recover 100sp.
